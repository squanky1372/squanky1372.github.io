What is the game?

This game is a fairly faithful recreation of the 2010 iOS game of the same name, Stardunk. I have a lot of fond memories playing this game. Sadly, it seems that the game is no longer playable through normal means, and its servers have shut down. Therefore, I took it upon myself to recreate the core game and as many of its mechanics as I could. Here are the rules:
  • You have 120 seconds to score as many points as possible.

  • Each basket you make has a base value of ten points, but can multiplied in several ways:
    • x2: Scoring a "clean" basket without touching the backboard
    • x3: Scoring an "extra clean" basket without touching the backboard or rim
    • x2: Fireball powerup active
    • xN: scoring N baskets in a row gives you a xN multiplier, up to a maximum of 10

  • Note: clean and extra clean point multipliers do not stack with each other, but everything else stacks!

  • The backboard is made of four sections. When the ball hits one of the sections, it will activate. Once all four are activated, you are granted a powerup for ten seconds! A random powerup is selected each time from the following options:
    • Wide hoop: the hoop expands to make clean and extra clean shots easier!
    • Triple ball: fire three balls at a time instead of one!
    • Fireball: earn twice as many points per shot!
